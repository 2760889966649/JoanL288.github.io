Fried Chicken

Fried chichken is a dish consisting of chicken pieces usually form broiler chickens which have been floured or battered and then pan-fried.
Ingredients
  • 4 pound chicken(cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups flour(for coating)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper(to tastes)
  • 2 quatys vegetable oils

Directions
  1. Put flour in a large plastiv bag, and season the flour with paprika, salt, and pepper to taste.
  2. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk.
  3. Put the chicken in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.
  4. Place chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with clean dish towel or waxed paper
  5. Let sit until the flour is of a paste-like consistency！！
  6. Fill large skilley about 1/3 to 1/2 full with vegetable oil. Heat until very hot.
  7. Put the chicken pieces in the skillet, and brown the chicken in hot oil on both sides.
  8. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes
  9. Remove cover, raise heat again and continue to fry until crispy
  10. Drain the chicken on paper towels
